About Us

We are a dedicated construction company with years of experience delivering high-quality projects. Dar Al Zain was founded in 2002, it was designed to provide services that cater to government and private companies in their needs of transportation vehicles and transportation related services. The company is offering heavy and light machinery, vehicles and equipment to support the construction, preparation and excavation of buildings, Houses, ways and high ways, commercial and private establishments across the emirates. Dar Al Zain has helped many companies such works as back filling, compaction, grading, transporting and supplying of different construction materials and aggregate that are needed in the sites.
